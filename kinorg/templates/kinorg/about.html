About Kinorg

Kinorg is a place to keep track of films you want to watch — together.

If you've ever tried to organise a film night with friends, you'll know the problem: everyone has films they want to see, but there's no easy way to pool them into a shared list. Kinorg fills that gap.

How it works

  1. Create a list — give it a name, it's yours.
  2. Search for films — add anything you want to watch.
  3. Invite friends — they can add and remove films too.

That's it. No fuss.

Whether it's a group movie night, a shared bucket list, or just keeping tabs on what your friends are into — Kinorg keeps it in one place.
